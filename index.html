My Thesis

Match balancing is one of the most important design issues in the development of a competitive multiplayer shooter. In this thesis we approach this issue creating maps aimed to balance differents player skills and strategies. Our maps are generated using genetics algorithms with a simulation-based fitness function. This fitness function evaluates results gained from matches played by two bots (with different skills/strategies) on the generated map. At the end, we shown that it's possible, using the procedural content generation, create maps that balance the different player skills/strategies.
